Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

The X-ray Bone Fracture Detection System is designed with privacy at its core. We understand the sensitive nature of medical information and have implemented strict measures to protect your data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

1. Image Data

What we collect:

  • X-ray images you upload for analysis
  • Image metadata (file size, format, dimensions)

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Patient names or personal identifiers
  • Medical record numbers
  • Date of birth or age
  • Contact information
  • Insurance information
  • Any HIPAA-protected health information (PHI)

2. Technical Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Access times and dates
  • Pages visited within the application

3. Usage Data

  • Number of analyses performed
  • General usage patterns
  • Error logs (if system issues occur)

Data Retention

Uploaded Images

Zero Retention Policy

All uploaded X-ray images are automatically deleted immediately after analysis is complete. Images are processed in real-time and are never permanently stored on our servers.

Temporary Storage

During analysis (typically 2-4 seconds), images are:

  • Stored in temporary memory only
  • Processed by the AI model
  • Deleted immediately upon completion
  • Never written to permanent storage

Technical Logs

Non-identifiable technical logs are retained for:

  • System maintenance and debugging: 30 days
  • Security monitoring: 90 days
  • All logs are anonymized and contain no medical information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

1. Providing the Service

  • Processing X-ray images through our AI model
  • Generating fracture detection results
  • Creating Grad-CAM visualizations
  • Displaying results to you

2. Improving the System

  • Monitoring system performance
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Improving AI model accuracy (using anonymized data only)

3. Security

  • Detecting and preventing unauthorized access
  • Protecting against malicious use
  • Ensuring system integrity

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your images with anyone
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Store your medical information

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

Technical Safeguards

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS (in production)
  • Secure Processing: Images processed in isolated environment
  • Access Control: Restricted system access with authentication
  • Regular Updates: Security patches applied promptly
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and logging

Physical Safeguards

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Restricted physical access to systems
  • Regular security audits

Administrative Safeguards

  • Privacy training for team members
  • Clear data handling procedures
  • Incident response protocols

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, or rent your personal information or uploaded images to any third parties.

Limited Exceptions

We may share anonymized, non-medical data only in these circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or legal process
  • Security Threats: To protect against fraud or security threats
  • Service Providers: With hosting providers (who cannot access medical data)

Note: Any shared data is strictly technical and contains no medical information, images, or personal identifiers.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

1. Right to Information

You have the right to know what data we collect and how we use it (this policy).

2. Right to Access

You can request information about any data we may have about you.

3. Right to Deletion

Since we don't store your images, this happens automatically after each analysis.

4. Right to Object

You can object to data processing by simply not using our service.

5. Right to Portability

Since we don't retain your data, there is nothing to export or transfer.

Cookies and Tracking

Session Cookies

We use minimal session cookies for:

  • Maintaining your session while using the application
  • Remembering your preferences during your visit

No Tracking

We do NOT use:

  • Third-party tracking cookies
  • Analytics cookies (like Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect functionality.

Children's Privacy

This service is intended for use by medical professionals and authorized personnel. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us immediately.

International Users

This service may be hosted in various locations. By using this service, you consent to the transfer of your data to the hosting location. We implement appropriate safeguards regardless of location.

GDPR (European Users)

If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to be forgotten (automatic - we don't store images)
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authority
